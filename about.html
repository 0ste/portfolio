I'm a Product Designer who connects UX, visual direction and design systems into digital products that feel both considered and expressive.

With 14+ years in design, my path towards product design led me from graphic design to design mentorship and agency roles, gradually shifting my focus to building digital products. This mix taught me to look at problems from different angles — from business goals and user needs to the visual language and systems that hold everything together. I'm comfortable navigating ambiguity, shaping end-to-end journeys and setting a clear design direction for teams.

Outside of work, I explore acting and singing, which keeps me close to storytelling and helps me bring more presence, empathy and nuance into how I design for different people.
